How the Platform works

• After milling, coffee is graded and packed ready for shipping

• We provide pre-finances to give farmer an advance payment and to cater for logistics

• Coffee is shipped to destination country and warehoused

• Profiles of the coffee are posted on the platform and samples are availed to buyers

• Buyers order coffee and pay directly to farmer's account

• The payment system first settles pre-financed amounts

• The remainder is paid to the farmer

• Nice Direct ships the coffee to buyers premises

• Information on the order including what ended up in the farmer's pocket is shared for all transacting parties

Bucket list (Reservation)

As a buyer, you can make reservations of coffees that are expected to be in stock in the future. All such reservations follow the criteria below:

Reservations for green coffees

- Non committal - No financial deposit needed.

- Subject to availability - Reservations are materialized once coffees are available.

- Notifications on availability - Notifications are sent via the platform’s notification system and email once coffees are available.

- Priority to purchase - Once bucket listed coffees are available, buyers with the coffee in their bucket list get priority of purchase.

- Priority on first-to-add first-serve basis - Priority to purchase bucket listed coffees is based on the date and time you added it to your bucket list in relation to other buyers.

- Priority stands for 72hrs from coffees arrival - When bucket listed coffees arrive and are placed in-stock, notifications are sent out immediately. From the time the notifications are sent out, your priority to purchase bucket listed coffees will stand for 72hrs after which it expires and can be purchased by any buyer.

Reservations for roasted coffees

- Non committal - No financial deposit needed.

- Subject to availability - Reservations are materialized once coffees are available.

- Notifications on availability - Notifications are sent via the platform’s notification system and email once coffees are available.
